What is the Deck Workshop?
The Deck Workshop is a place for you to create custom cards to use in a card duel against your friends! Here, we'll let you set up your own question, which will be auto-magically turned into a card. This card will then be added to your deck!

Have suggestions? We'd love to hear from you! Simply click on the orange tab on the lower right of the page to tell us of your ideas.

Click on View Inventory to look at your current collections of cards, or click on Add Questions to build a Card now!
